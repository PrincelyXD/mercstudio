Our Story

Merc Studio was founded in 2020 in Birmingham, England . Whilst we still work with our favourite brands, in 2022 we moved our headquarters to Oxford.

We are an agile and dedicated boutique design and visual communications agency. Every day we work with people and brands that want to stand for something and make a visual impact.

Something new. Something big. Something special.

We work as an exclusive partner with our clients, creating, reinvigorating and maintaining their brand visuals; whether digitally, for events, print media or product packaging. We ensure consistency, strengthening a brand visually and differentiating it from the competition.

For extended expertise Merc Studio works in partnership with our trusted partners to include content and channel strategies; and targeted marketing solutions for the rapidly changing digital environment.
